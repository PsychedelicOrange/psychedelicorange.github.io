Three sources of income

The three sources of income are

  1. Earnings : Money you get for the work you did. Includes bonuses, tips, etc.
  2. Rent : Money you get from already owned wealth ( at no extra work ).
  3. Investments : Money you get for the money you risked/used. Meaning money from stock trading, buisness investments,etc.
