Why I started blogging

I never considered the idea of writing blogs, and thought it was very antique. In a world where each activity is competing for your attention, who would make time to read these sentences? It was only when I came across blogs by IT professionals about niche computers topics, each providing information that is hard to find - detailing their processes, understanding, knowledge, I saw some value in blogs/ websites. Novelty of finding a rare/new website online is a missed experience – [Sort by new] is now an optional filter , [Sort by Popular] being the default. So I want to make a cool and quirky website. And write about stuff I learn.
